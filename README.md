# Privacy Policy for WA Toolkit AI

**Last Updated: May 29, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how "WA Toolkit AI" ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to ensuring user privacy and maintaining data security baseline standards required by the Google Play Store.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
Our application functions primarily as a local backend utility and automation toolkit. 
* **Personal Data:** We do not explicitly collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number to external cloud servers.
* **Device and Usage Data:** We may collect non-identifying diagnostic logs or performance analytics solely to optimize app functionality and monitor runtime crash rates.

## 2. Third-Party Services and Permissions
To provide specific automated utilities, our app interacts with core platform features. We utilize trusted third-party tools (such as Google Play Services) which may process data according to their respective policies.

* **Core Interactions:** Any local scraping or processing of messaging notifications stays entirely sandbox-isolated on your physical device. No message content is uploaded to external platforms.
* **Permissions:** We enforce a "minimal data collection" architecture. In alignment with recent Google Play guidelines, if you interact with local address book features, we utilize the native Android Contact Picker system-level UI rather than requesting broad, blanket background contact permissions.

## 3. Data Safety & Secure Handling
We take the security of your data seriously:
* Any information processed locally by the application is protected using standard on-device sandbox encryption layers.
* Data is not shared with third-party advertising networks or data brokers.

## 4. User Rights and Data Deletion
Since we do not host user accounts or sync personal data to external storage clusters, there is no permanent user profile retained on our systems. If you wish to clear all cached application history, you can simply clear the application storage data directly from your device's Android System Settings or uninstall the app.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy periodically to reflect new system optimizations or compliance modifications. You are advised to review this page periodically for any updates.
