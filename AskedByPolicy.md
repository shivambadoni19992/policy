Privacy Policy for AskedBy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to AskedBy.

AskedBy is an interview preparation application that helps users discover coding problems and view companies that have previously asked those questions.

Information We Collect

AskedBy is designed to minimize data collection.

Information Stored Locally

The app may store the following information on your device:

Problem completion status
Done
Revising
Not Done
Favorite questions
Search preferences
Solution images uploaded by the user
App settings

This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Solution Images

Users may optionally attach images of their solutions, notes, or explanations to coding problems.

These images:

Are stored locally on your device.
Are not uploaded to our servers.
Are not shared with third parties.
Can be deleted by the user at any time.

Advertisements may collect certain information automatically, including:

Device information

App usage information
